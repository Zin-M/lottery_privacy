Privacy Policy

Thai Lottery Results
Last Updated: December 10, 2025

Summary: Thai Lottery Results is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your data stays on your device.

Introduction

Welcome to Thai Lottery Results ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information Collection

We want to be clear: we do not collect any personal information from you.

  • We do not require account registration
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data

Local Data Storage

The App stores some data locally on your device to enhance your experience:

  • Favorite Numbers: Numbers you save are stored only on your device
  • App Preferences: Your language and theme settings are stored locally
  • Cached Results: Recent lottery results are cached for offline viewing

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Data Source

Lottery results displayed in the App are fetched from publicly available data provided by the Thai Government Lottery Office (GLO) through their Open Data API. We simply display this publicly available information and do not modify or manipulate the data.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect certain information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before allowing AdMob to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites.

  • If you allow tracking: You may see personalized ads based on your interests
  • If you deny tracking: You will still see ads, but they will not be personalized to your interests

Children's Privacy

This App is rated 17+ and is not intended for children under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App displays lottery results for informational purposes and is designed for adult users only.

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data at risk. All locally stored preferences and favorites are protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption when your device is locked.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Data: Use the "Clear Cache" option in Settings to remove all cached data
  • Remove Favorites: Delete individual favorite numbers at any time
  • Uninstall: Removing the App deletes all locally stored data
  • Ad Preferences: Control ad personalization through iOS Settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
